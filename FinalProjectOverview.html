Application Overview

This application will be to help anyone choose there perfect starter pokemon from all the regions in pokemon. Growing up as a kid, I would struggle to choose the right starter pokemon for myslef. With this you can choose the one that best fits you! It will just have a few questions that will help in identifying your best starter!

Features In The Program

  • Questions that will need to be answered to figure out the best pokemon for you!
  • Will have you pick which reigion you want to pick starters from or just have a general pick of all the regional starters.
  • Might include what your perfect starter says about you if I can as well.
